Sword Fish Project

A creative hybrid model of a sword and a fish created using Maya, showcasing advanced modeling and rendering techniques.

Highlights

  • Used polygon modeling, NURBS, and curves for detailed shapes
  • Applied Arnold Physical Sky and Directional Light for natural lighting
  • Utilized UV mapping and metallic shaders for realistic texturing
  • Rendered high-quality stills at 1920x1080 resolution

Technologies Used

  • Autodesk Maya
  • Arnold Renderer
  • UV Mapping
